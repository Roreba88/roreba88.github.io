Junjie's Biography

  • Since October 2016, I have been a PhD student at the University of Groningen, Groningen, the Netherlands, working with Prof. Harry L. Trentelman and Prof. M. Kanat Camlibel.

  • I received the M.Eng. degree in Control Science and Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, in 2016.

  • I received the B.Eng. degree in Automation from Anhui University of Technology, Anhui, in 2013.

  • I was born in July 1993, in Lu'an, Anhui, China.
