Bio Sketch


Rodolfo Reyes-Báez was born in Puebla Mexico, in 1988. He received the B.Sc. degree in Mechatronics Engineering from the Meritorious Autonomous University of Puebla (BUAP), Puebla, Mexico, in 2011; the M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering (Mechatronics) from the Center for Research and Advanced Studies (CINVESTAV), Mexico City, Mexico, in 2014; and the Ph.D. degree in Systems and Control from the University of Groningen (RuG), The Netherlands, in 2019.
From Jan. 2019 to Jul. 2020, he was a research control engineer with TNO Wind Energy, Petten, The Netherlands. He received the Roberto Rocca fellowship Doctorate Awardee in 2018. Since July 2020, he is a research and teaching fellow at the Faculty of Science and Engineering of the University of Groningen. His current research interests include: Lyapunov, incremental stability and passivity techniques in nonlinear control, energy-based modeling and control, robotics and mechatronics, and modern engineering applications
